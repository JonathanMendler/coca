Coheed & Cambria
An Auditory Saga

Sometimes you just listen to music. It's something to throw on in the background while you're preparing dinner, cleaning out the dungeon, or whittling a tiny beluga whale out of piece of wood you've found whilst sitting on the porch awaiting the arrival of the impending storm. Then other times, you experience it; And that is just what Coheed & Cambria have set out to do.

The Chronicles

Coheed & Cambria has gone through many iterations of itself before becoming what it is today. A plethora of names & members since its inception in 1995, it wasn't until a trip to Paris in 1998 that lead singer Claudio Sanchez began work on his science-fiction comics called The.On.Line.Adventures, which would later become known as The Amory Wars. It was here that it was decided the band would be called Coheed & Cambria, the namesake of the two main characters. The music would take on a more progressive rock structure & become a conceptual art form; In essence, it would become the soundtrack to the story.

The Amory Wars

The Amory Wars is set in Heaven’s Fence, a collection of 78 planets and seven stars, held in place by interconnecting beams of energy known as the “Keywork”.





"Year of the Black Rainbow" and "Second Stage Turbine Blade" narrate the struggles of Coheed and Cambria Kilgannon against Wilhelm Ryan, the Supreme Tri-Mage who has launched a war campaign with the intended goal to rule over Heaven’s Fence.

The Crowing



The story arcs of "In Keeping Secrets of Silent Earth: 3", "Good Apollo, I’m Burning Star IV: Vol. 1 – From Fear Through The Eyes of Madness", and "Good Apollo, I’m Burning Star IV: Vol. 2 – No World For Tomorrow", focus on the heroic journey of Claudio Kilgannon, son of Coheed and Cambria, and his journey to assume the mantle of The Crowing, the foretold savior of Heaven’s Fence.

and
The Keywork



Set long before the events of the previous chapters, "The Afterman: Ascension" and "The Afterman: Descension" tell the story of Dr. Sirius Amory, a scientist who enters the Keywork in an attempt to understand its mysterious energy.

"Vaxis – Act I: The Unheavenly Creatures" is the first of five story arcs that continue the original Amory Wars saga and follows the story of Creature and Sister Spider, who are incarcerated within the prison planet known as The Dark Sentencer. With the recent release of "Vaxis - Act II: A Window of the Waking Mind", we're sure to see more of the story unveil itself in the near future.
